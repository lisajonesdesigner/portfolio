.img · block, 100% width.   .img-border · adds 1px grey border.   .img-zoom · hover scale 2.2× (parent needs .grid-overflow).

14 · Hero   CSS section 15: Hero

Full-bleed image hero with overlay. Used at the top of all case study pages.

.hero → .hero-bg (img) + .hero-overlay (div) + .hero__inner (div) → h1
