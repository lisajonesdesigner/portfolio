4 · Shadows   CSS section 1: Tokens, plus per-component declarations

Shadows are used sparingly: two text-shadows (tokenised) for hero text legibility, and four one-off box-shadows for elevation on interactive or overlay elements. No card in section 3 above carries a shadow — elevation there comes from background/border only.

Hero title text
--shadow-text-sm · 0 1px 3px rgba(0,0,0,0.6) — paired with an inline 0 6px 24px rgba(0,0,0,0.4) on .hero-title for legibility over a photo. Shown here on light blue so the shadow itself is visible; in real use it sits on a photo, not a flat colour.
