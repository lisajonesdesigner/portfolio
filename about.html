About

Senior Product Designer.
Regulated health, aged care,
and education platforms.

My work focuses on platforms where structure, policy, and accountability shape the experience as much as user needs. I design calm, predictable systems for people working under pressure, with decisions grounded in clear responsibility boundaries, defensible interaction logic, and evidence traceability.

I specialise in environments where failure carries risk, including healthcare, aged care, and higher education platforms operating under regulatory, audit, and accessibility constraints. Recent work includes FHIR-aligned clinical and care systems, assessor-led funding reform, and institution-wide learning platforms, delivered in close partnership with product, engineering, and domain experts.

I bring a strong foundation in visual design and accessibility, informed by 20 years across branding, advertising, and design agencies. This supports clear hierarchy, restraint, and legibility across complex workflows rather than surface polish.

Postgraduate study in digital learning design informs how systems guide users through unfamiliar or high-stakes tasks. This strengthens onboarding and in-product guidance while reducing misuse, variation, and operational risk.

I work best where systems need to scale, rules matter, and clarity is non-negotiable.
